About the Project

What is ChemArch?

The ChemArch project presents an intelligent QSAR (Quantitative Structure–Activity Relationship) based system designed to predict chemical behavior and potential outcomes of molecular combinations using computational modeling.

The Problem

Why Computational Chemistry?

Traditional laboratory experimentation in chemistry and pharmaceuticals demands significant time, cost, and resources. Computational approaches offer faster, scalable, and cost-effective alternatives for chemical analysis and drug discovery.

Technology

System Architecture

PubChem Integration

Molecular data is fetched in real-time using the PubChem API, ensuring accurate and up-to-date chemical information.

Descriptor Extraction

Key molecular descriptors such as Molecular Weight, LogP, Hydrogen Bond Donors and Acceptors are extracted for analysis.

QSAR Modeling

Quantitative Structure–Activity Relationship models are applied to predict chemical behavior and biological activity.

Prediction Engine

The system processes molecular data through trained logic to generate accurate predictive insights.

Visualization

Results are presented through interactive and easy-to-understand visual outputs for better interpretation.
